TRANSUMANZA

SANTA CECILIA & SEMIONAUTA TRASCENDENZE ARTIFICIALI ECOSISTEMI ARTIFICIALI

Transumanza is an open-research project based on pastoral folklore and contemporary music.

The project started in Sicily as a 40-day journey, during which the artists traveled and lived in close contact with local artisans, musicians, and local communities. This intimate dialogue culminated in a residency at the Museo Ibleo delle Arti e Mestieri in Modica for SITU Festival 2024, supported by the Municipality. Transumanza was later presented during MANIFEST:IO Festival in Berlin in February 2025 as well as in other locations across Italy.
