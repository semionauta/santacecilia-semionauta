SANTA CECILIA & SEMIONAUTA TRANSUMANZA ECOSISTEMI ARTIFICIALI

TRASCENDENZE ARTIFICIALI

Trascendenze Artificiali explores immersive, transmedial experiences where music, light, body, and technology converge to create dreamlike spaces and parallel dimensions.

The project began as a workshop-based durational performance, first presented at Fabbrica Europa Festival 2023 in Florence and further developed during an art residency at NUB Project Space, Pistoia. It evolved across multiple iterations at venues including Associazione Culturale Dello Scompiglio, ultimately culminating in a vinyl release on the Netherlands-based record label Strange Therapy. Trascendenze Artificiali has fostered collaborations in music, fashion, and performance, including Demetrio Cecchitelli, Kasei Archive, Alessia De Francesco, Marianna De Vito, Linda Favero, Eleonora Gambini, Alessandra Indolfi, Roberta Indolfi, Sofia Kaloterakis, Eleonora Marzani, Marinella Melegari, and Eva Luna Thomann.

“Trascendenze Artificiali: Act I,” the album, reworks the sonic material of the original performance, creating an immersive auditory landscape that mirrors the live experience while standing as a work in its own right. Through guttural vocalizations, experimental electronic textures, industrial beats, and doom-influenced atmospheres, the work navigates themes of identity fragmentation, the tension between organic and synthetic realities, and the dissolution of meaning. Hypnotic chants, layered feedback, and custom-built instruments craft a soundscape that oscillates between orchestral arrangements and freeform structures, offering an experience both intimate and monolithic.

The live performances themselves are arenas where sound, body, architecture, and technology collide, forming ecosystems of disorientation, ritual, and raw emotion. This energy translates into the recordings, where extended durations, repetitions, and physical intensity become a language of their own—an evolving, liminal journey refusing conventional resolution. Each listener is invited to enter, exit, and re-enter this sonic space, shaping a personal path through thresholds, altered states, and shared experiences.

Trascendenze Artificiali exists as a hybrid ecosystem—performance, recording, and ongoing research—where audience, artists, and environment converge to generate continuously evolving narrative tensions, unifying diverse perspectives both aesthetically and conceptually.
